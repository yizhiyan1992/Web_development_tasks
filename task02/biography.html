About me

I am currently an Associate Professor in the Department of Civil & Environmental Engineering at the University of Utah. My research specialty lies in sustainable transportation system with a focus on public transit, managed lanes, large-scale transportation system modeling and simulation, GIS-based infrastructure asset management, network complexity of social sciences, and Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS). I am affiliated with Utah Traffic Lab here at the U. I got my Ph.D. in Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Washington where I was affiliated with Smart Transportation Applications and Research Laboratory (STAR Lab), and worked as a transportation engineering intern in the urban planning office of the Washington State Department of Transportation (WSDOT). Before joining the UW, I studied at Beijing Jiaotong University where I got my Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Electrical Engineering, and Texas Southern University where I got my Master's degree in Transportation Planning and Management.

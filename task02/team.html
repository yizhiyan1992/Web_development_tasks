Team

I joined the University of Utah since 2013. Our team conducted extensive research in sustainable transportation system with a focus on shared mobility, public transit, managed lanes, large-scale transportation modeling and simulation, GIS-based infrastructure asset management, network complexity of social sciences, and Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS). So far, five students have successfully graduated from our team. They get their jobs in both industry and academia.
